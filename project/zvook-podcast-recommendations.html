Project brief & my role

Zvook.co is a New York-based startup established to match brands with podcasts. As a Product Development Intern, I was responsible for developing NLP-based algorithms that analyzes podcast data to recommend a list of potential podcasts based on brand alignment, style, target group and relevance.
