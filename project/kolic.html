Project brief & my role

The first Korean Language International Debate Championships (KOLIC) is a tournament hosted by Debate Korea. KOLIC aspires to serve as the venue of communication where Korean-speaking university students from all over the world can gather to discuss current affairs and exchange culture through the Korean language. Sponsored by the Korean Ministry of Education, KOLIC will gather leading global leaders from all over the world to form an open community that communicates and competes with each other, laying the foundation for a continuous network of cultural and academic exchanges between Korean and overseas university students using Korean as a medium. As a lead UX designer and Front-End developer, I was responsible for designing a website for the tournament.
